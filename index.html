ABOUT

This Apr 29- May 1, Hack Davis will bring together over 600 of the nation's most promising developers and designers to create incredible software and hardware projects. Meet fellow hackers, learn new technologies, and hone your skills alongside seasoned mentors. Whether you're making your first mobile app or are ready to jump onboard the most developed hardware track in the country, join us for 36 glorious hours of learning.

Hack Davis will be taking place on the beautiful campus of UC Davis in the Walter A. Buehler Alumni Center.
